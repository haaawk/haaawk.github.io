--- layout: post title: "ScyllaDB/Cassandra SSTable format" subtitle: "Variant integers encoding" date: 2018-02-26 10:30:25 +0100 ---

SSTable format uses a concept of variant integers borrowed from Protocol Buffers Base 128 Variants. Encoding isn't exactly the same but it shares the idea that an integer does not have to be represented by a fixed number of bytes. In SSTables, a variant integer can be represented by 1-9 bytes. The length of the representation is encoded in the first byte.
